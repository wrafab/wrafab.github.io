1. Using Fusion 360, create your own furniture. You can see an example video of how you may create it here.

OR

2. Create your own 3D printed hinge. We will 3D print the hinge when complete. You can see an example of how you may create it here or here.

OR

3. Create an snapfit case. It must be your original design and not a recreation of the video tutorial. You can see an example of how you may create it here.

OR

4. Create a laser cut laptop stand. You can see an example of how you may create it here..

AND

You must document your choice using the Class Page. You should include 2 pictures and the Fusion 360 original files on your page. Illustrate what works and did not work when designing the enclosure. It is important to reflect on your success and failure.
