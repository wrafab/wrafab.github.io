Assignments

1. Design an original T-Shirt and print it on the Brother GT-361 printer. It must be an original design and cannot use any copyrighted materials. Make sure to include your original files, pictures of the process and finished product.


AND

2. UV print a 12" x12" white chipboard on the Roland LEF-20 UV printer. Using your CAD skills on Fusion360, create profile lines for a jigsaw puzzle. You can see a tutorial about it here. In your page files, talk about the limitations of the printer and what types of items the LEF-20 could be used to print on. Include your files and pictures to document the process. We will then proceed to laser cut chipboard to create your custom jigsaw puzzle.

AND

3. You Must Select One Of The Following


A. Print a logo on a golf ball

B. Print a phone case

C. Print a Paper Mache box top


AND

4. You also will need to complete your dye sublimination project on the Sawgrass 800 printer. In your portfolio, discuss how the process of dye sublimation works and what advantages does dye sublimation hold over UV printing. Include your original files and pictures of the final dye sublimination project.
