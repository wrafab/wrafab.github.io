Wearables

Wearables or e-texiles are the mixture of electronics and wearable items and fashion. It is an emerging market with embedded sensors, lights, motors and small microprocessors. You can be creative with conductive materials like thread and cloth. We will briefly explore e-textiles and wearables by creating a few simple projects using leds, conductive thread, batteries and cloth.
